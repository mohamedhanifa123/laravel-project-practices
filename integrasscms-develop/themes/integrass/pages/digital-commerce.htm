Digital Commerce

Technology has changed the face of retail, shifting the power from brands to consumers who are well informed and have more options. It has shaken up the marketplace, allowing the most determined competitors to emerge as the new leaders.

By 2020, digital commerce is expected to grow exponentially to reach US$32 billion. The driving force behind all these changes is the consumer. As life gets more hectic, consumers want to be rid of time-consuming activities and engage in activities they value most. With the best technologies literally in the palm of their hands, consumers expect seamless customer experiences online and offline.

Business today embrace the new age of digital commerce to meet the growing demands of the evolving consumer.
