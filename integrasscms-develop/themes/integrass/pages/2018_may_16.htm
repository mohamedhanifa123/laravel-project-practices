Integrass expands services, leads to planned job growth.

Orlando, FL (1888PressRelease) May 17, 2018 - Across Borders Management Consulting Group, a rapidly growing, award-winning technology solutions company, is now Integrass.

The global rebrand allows the company to bring together its operations in the United States, India and Australia under one umbrella to better serve clients.

“As Integrass, we are able to truly unite our branches across the globe, bringing our strengths together and creating economies of scale,” said Managing Partner Arathi Rajagopalan. “We will be able to serve clients 24/7, streamlining communications and allowing us to deliver next-level results.”

Because the rebranding of the company brings together its global resources, Integrass will also be able to offer clients innovative solutions in fields like artificial intelligence and IOT.

Integrass is a technology solutions provider that creates customized, end-to-end IT solutions to give clients a competitive edge. The company offers app development, product engineering, cloud services, automation, assurance and testing, infrastructure management, and smart sourcing. It also offers a number of easily deployable products, such as a Human Resource Management platform that supports sales.

“At its core, Integrass is innovative,” said Srinivas V, vice president of global delivery. “As one company, we will be able to focus on our strengths and push the envelope, utilizing open source and cloud technologies to their full extent. That means we can deliver a better, more secure product to clients more quickly and less expensively so they can stay on the cutting edge.”

The company also anticipates significant global growth over the next year. Integrass plans to hire for 30 positions worldwide, with 15 in the U.S.

Integrass is woman and minority owned. The company has received numerous accolades for its incredible growth, including being named to the Inc. 5000 and to the Orlando Business Journal’s Fast 50 three times.
