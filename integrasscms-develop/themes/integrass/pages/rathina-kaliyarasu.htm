Rathina Kaliyarasu

Practice Head – Software Engineering

Rathina Kaliyarasu has over 15 years of experience in the IT industry and is skilled in Cloud Computing, Information Management and Applications Development, Business Application Integration, and Service Activation. He invests his experience in the areas of Architecting, Designing, and delivering innovative business solutions for clients.

Rathina has worked for global software leaders like Verizon, Tech-Mahindra, HPS, etc. and delivered complex application systems in a demanding schedule. He has earned a Master’s Degree in Information Technology from the Madras University, Chennai, India. He also earned the “First Rank” in his university for his distinguished academic performance.
