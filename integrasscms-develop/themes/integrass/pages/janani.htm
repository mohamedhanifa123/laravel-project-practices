Janani Sekar

Technical Delivery Manager

Janani handles multiple clients at the Integrass Sports business unit, where she invests her technical expertise, gained over 9 years of experience. She has a proven track record of managing the functions within her team, and provide regular direction, ensuring the success of the team’s manual and automation efforts.

She has worked with Cognizant, one of the fortune 500 companies, where she delivered slippage-free projects with optimal effort. She has also gained wide experience in strategic thinking and planning and provides expertise throughout the entire product development life cycle with a strong sense of quality ownership making sure that quality is baked in from the start.

She has earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering in the stream of Electronics and Communications, with distinction, under Anna University, Chennai.
