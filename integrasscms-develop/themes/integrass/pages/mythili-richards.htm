Mythili Richards

Director, Finance

Mythili Richards has been such an asset to the ABMCG Team. She started with the company in September 2016.

Mythili has several years of experience in the Accounting area, she not only handles the payroll but also plays an intricate role in dealing with the company taxes and finances.

She is a Certified Enrolled Agent for the Department of The Treasury for the IRS. Mythili continues to bring her expertise in her field to the health and growth of our company.
