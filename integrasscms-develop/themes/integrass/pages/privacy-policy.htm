YOUR CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA. While this Privacy Policy comprehensively describes how Integrass uses personal information about you, the CCPA provides some California residents the opportunity to exercise specific rights relating to data transparency, outlined below.

Right to know

At your request, Integrass will inform you of the categories of personal information we collect about you and the purposes for which those categories of personal information are used for the 12-month period preceding your request, including the following:

  1. The categories of personal information we have collected about you;
  2. The categories of sources from which your personal information is collected;
  3. The business or commercial purpose for collecting or selling your personal information;
  4. The categories of third parties with whom we share your personal information; and
  5. The specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.

Right to delete

You also have the right to request that Integrass deletes any personal information about you that we have collected from you. Integrass will respond to a verifiable request from you to delete your data, except in circumstances where it is necessary for us to maintain your personal information in order to:

  1. - Complete the transaction for which your personal information was collected, provide a good or service requested by you or reasonably anticipated within the context of our ongoing business relationship with you, or otherwise perform a contract between us and you;
  2. - Detect security incidents, protect against malicious, deceptive, fraudulent, or illegal activity; or prosecute those responsible for that activity;
  3. - Debug our systems to identify and repair errors that impair existing intended functionality;
  4. - Exercise free speech, ensure the right of another consumer to exercise his or her right of free speech, or exercise another right provided for by law;
  5. - Comply with the California Electronic Communications Privacy Act pursuant to Chapter 3.6 (commencing with Section 1546) of Title 12 of Part 2 of the Penal Code;
  6. - If you have provided informed consent, engage in public or peer-reviewed scientific, historical, or statistical research in the public interest that adheres to all other applicable ethics and privacy laws, when our deletion of the information is likely to render impossible or seriously impair the achievement of such research;
  7. - To enable solely internal uses that are reasonably aligned with your expectations your relationship with us; or
  8. - Comply with a legal obligation.

To submit a request under your right to know and right to delete, you must email support@integrass.com. From the time Integrass verifies the request, we will respond within 45 days, and if additional time is needed, we will communicate extensions with you.

Other rights

You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising any of the rights listed above.
