Vijay Krishnan

Executive Board Director

Vijay Krishnan oversees strategy, service delivery and business development for Integrass worldwide.

Krishnan co-founded Integrass (Formerly Across Borders Management Consulting Group) along with Arathi Rajagopalan in the Fall of 2007. Prior to founding Integrass, Krishnan developed software delivery and project management expertise at Ultramatics, a Premier IBM Partner company he co-founded in 2004, and managed delivery at Fortune 500 companies like Fiserv and Motorola as Director of Project Management and Senior Software Engineer respectively.
