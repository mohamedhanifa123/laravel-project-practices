Ramprasad Rajaraman

Head Microsoft Practice & Architect

Ram comes with extensive experience in Systems Integration across Enterprise and Cloud systems and provides innovative ways of architecting the key integration component across various business solutions for clients.

Ram is highly skilled in the design/development of Enterprise Applications, Systems Integrations and Cloud Computing with both Microsoft .NET and Amazon AWS stack of technologies and tools. He worked for the global software leaders like HPE, Symantec, Unisys and has been key team member in Systems Integration and Application Modernization across the enterprise.

Ram has B.E in Computer Science and Engineering from Bharathidasan University, Trichy and bagged a Patent Publishment and Technology Innovation Award during his tenure in HPE.
