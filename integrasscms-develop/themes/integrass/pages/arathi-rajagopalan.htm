Arathi Rajagopalan

Chief Operating Officer & President

Arathi Rajagopalan oversees Client Engagement, Service Delivery, and Employee Welfare for Integrass worldwide.

She has co-founded Integrass (Formerly Across Borders Management Consulting Group) in the Fall of 2007. Prior to founding Integrass, she managed delivery at Fortune 500 companies like Fiserv, Verizon, and Gentiva Health Services respectively.

Rajagopalan holds a Masters of Computer Engineering & Nano Technology from the University of Texas, El Paso, TX, and earned her Bachelors's degree in Electronics from India. She is a certified Project Management Professional. She is passionate about helping others and taking part in charitable works.
