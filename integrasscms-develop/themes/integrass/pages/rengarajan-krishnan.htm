Rengarajan Krishnan

Vice President – India Operations and Board Director

Rengarajan Krishnan leads the Operations of the Global Service including the Human Resource, Administration, and Finance program at Integrass. Rengarajan has been a Board Director since the inception of Integrass Technical Services Pvt. Ltd.

Rengarajan is an accomplished leader with 25 years of operations experience and managing large teams in the Financial and Banking sectors with strong Administrative, Financial, and Operational Acumen and the ability to build operational efficiencies and growth through effective administration. Rengarajan holds a Bachelor’s Degree with a specialization in Commerce.
