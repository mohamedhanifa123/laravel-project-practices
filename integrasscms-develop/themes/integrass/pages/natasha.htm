Nithya Muralidharan (Natasha)

Support Delivery Manager

Nithya Muralidharan (Natasha) is currently supporting many clients with the Learning Management System and is also sharing her expertise with VirtualOps IT Services, to enable distinct results, and to leverage business success to the organization.

She has more than 5 years of experience working with Multinational Financial corporations such as BNP Paribas Securities Services, Chennai, in managing projects and supporting global customers.

She earned her Master’s Degree in Business Administration from ITM (Institute for Technology and Management), and her Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce, with a specialization in Banking and Financial Support Services from Ethiraj college.
