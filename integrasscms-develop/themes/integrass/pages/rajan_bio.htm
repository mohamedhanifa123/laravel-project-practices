Raj Ramaswamy

Chief Financial Officer & Senior Vice President

Raj Ramaswamy has joined Integrass as its Chief Financial Officer and Senior Vice President focusing on monetization, strategy and diversification. Raj is a seasoned wealth, investment, finance & business management professional with 15+ years of experience. Before starting his own wealth management firm - Endovia, Raj has held senior leadership roles with JP Morgan, SBI Mutual Fund, Aditya Birla Sun Life and ICICI Prudential.

Raj hold a MBA from Drexel University, Philadelphia (USA) and Masters in Computer Application and Bachelors in Commerce from India. Raj is passionate about technology, investment management and innovations aiding social growth.
