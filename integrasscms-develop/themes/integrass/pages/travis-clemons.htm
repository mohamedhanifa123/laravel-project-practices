Travis Clemons

Senior Director, Sales & Marketing

Travis Clemons joined the Integrass team in March 2020. Travis is an experienced Marketing, Business Development, and Sales Manager with a background in technology, SaaS, financial, telecommunications, education, human resource predictive analytics and artificial intelligence service industries.

Travis holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) - with an emphasis in Business Development Marketing Management from the University of Phoenix and a Bachelor of Business Administration - Marketing from Belmont University in Nashville, Tennessee.
