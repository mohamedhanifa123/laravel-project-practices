Across Borders announces new business development director

Mike Sunners will help build on the company’s outstanding growth

MOUNT DORA, Fla. (May 10, 2017) — Across Borders, a global technology solutions provider, has hired Mike Sunners as business development director.

In the role, Sunners will focus on bringing in new customers, particularly those who can take advantage of the company’s customized, end-to-end IT solutions. He will also help manage existing accounts.

Sunners has more than 25 years’ experience and has facilitated growth at both high tech companies and startups. He previously worked as the vice president of business development at Dreifus Associates LTD., Inc, in Lake Mary, Fla. He has also served as the U.S. General Manager of Aspects Systems, Inc., and as General Manager of Kineticon.

“Mike not only has experience facilitating outstanding growth at companies of all sizes, he has a solid background in the IT field,” said Arathi Rajagopalan, Managing Partner. “We’re excited to see what he can do for our growing business both here in the U.S. and around the globe.”

“It’s obvious that Across Borders has had incredible success in the field thus far,” Sunners said. “I look forward to helping the business bring on clients and enter a new stage of growth.”

Sunners has a bachelor’s degree in engineering from the University of Edinburgh in Scotland. In his free time, he enjoys hiking and cycling and has trekked the foothills of the Himalayas and climbed Mt. Kilimanjaro.

For more information on Across Borders Management Consulting Group LLC dba Integrass, visit www.integrass.com.

About Across Borders Management Consulting Group LLC dba Integrass

Across Borders Management Consulting Group LLC dba Integrass is a global technology solutions provider that offers clients both customized, end-to-end IT solutions and products that are ready to launch through its product division, Fobess. Across Borders has expertise in the finance, health care and retail fields. Its products make it easy to launch everything from food delivery businesses and custom T-shirt design to job websites. The company, which began operations in 2011, is woman and minority owned and based in Mount Dora, Fla. Across Borders has been honored numerous times for its outstanding growth. It has been named to the Inc. 5000 list three times and made the Orlando Business Journal’s Fast 50 in 2016 and 2017. It has also been named among Florida Companies to Watch by GrowFL.
