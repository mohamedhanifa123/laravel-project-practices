COVID-19 Impact on Social and Emotional Learning

Learning Management Systems are a great boon for continuous education and skill development. This White Paper has been prudently written to address the emotional trauma of children, and the social impacts when the schools and colleges are closed owing to the pandemic.

This White Paper would also brief about how the LMS platform has helped children during COVID, and how it will alter the education and learning in the future, and give tremendous opportunities.

  • The Global Education Coalition
