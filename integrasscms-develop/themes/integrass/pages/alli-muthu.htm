Allimuthu Thangavel

Practice Head - Consulting Services

Allimuthu Thangavel joined the Integrass Team in Feb 2015 and directs the Technical Recruitment Team and Sales. He has over 14 years of experience and has gained rich professional expertise, working in the field of IT staffing and recruitment services.

He comes with the following credentials of a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Management and a Master’s Degree in Information science and Management. He also holds a Master’s Degree in Fashion Marketing Management from Istituto Europeo di Design (IED), Italy.
