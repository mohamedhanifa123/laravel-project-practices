USPAACC 2020 Awards

US Pan Asian American Chamber of Commerce has selected Integrass as one of the 100 fastest-growing companies of Asian origin. This is an annual recognition to put the companies in the spotlight for their achievement, and growth in the US. Integrass is among the minority, and women-owned business, and has shown tremendous growth over the past 11 years with over 110+ employees globally. Currently, they are serving under three diverse portfolios of Integrass Consulting, Integrass Virtual Ops, and Integrass Sport.

The company has made a significant impact on other business owners, companies, and several industries through their digital platforms, tools, and services. They are headquartered in Orlando, Florida, the other two office locations in the US are at Colorado, Texas, and Mount Dora, Florida. The other three offices are located across Pacific and Asian regions, strategically placed to cater to the growing global needs.
