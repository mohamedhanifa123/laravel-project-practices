Across Borders Management Consulting Group LLC dba Integrass honored among top Central Florida technology Companies

Orlando Business Journal selects firm for Innovations in Technology Awards

MOUNT DORA, Fla. (Nov. 8, 2017) – Across Borders Management Consulting Group LLC dba Integrass recently received the Orlando Business Journal’s Innovations in Technology Award for its outstanding contributions to the industry in Central Florida.

The awards honor both individuals and companies that embrace technology to positively impact both the top and bottom lines. They are open to small, medium and large companies, as well as to nonprofits, government and research organizations and those in education, health care and tourism. Companies must have a physical location in Orange, Osceola, Seminole, Lake, Brevard or Volusia counties.

Across Borders was among 17 businesses and two individuals to receive the award.

“At Across Borders, we’re all about technology,” said Managing Partner Arathi Rajagopalan. “But we aren’t just looking for the next big thing. We think about the ways technology can help people and businesses move forward and reach their goals. That’s what these awards are all about, and we’re honored to be recognized by OBJ.”

Across Borders is a technology solutions provider that implements customized, end-to-end IT solutions to help businesses expand and become user-friendly. Across Borders has delivered quality consulting solutions to many clients, including Fortune 500 companies such as Microsoft, Walgreens, Citibank and Toyota.

The company is woman and minority owned.

Across Borders has been growing and expanding rapidly. In 2017, the company was named to the Inc. 5000 for the third time in a row. It was also named to OBJ’s list of the 50 fastest growing companies for the second year in a row. Additionally, the company recently entered the South Florida and Atlanta markets.

For more information on Across Borders Management Consulting Group LLC dba Integrass, visit www.integrass.com.

About Across Borders Management Consulting Group LLC dba Integrass

Across Borders Management Consulting Group LLC dba Integrass is a global technology solutions provider that offers clients both customized, end-to-end IT solutions and products that are ready to launch through its product division, Fobess. Across Borders has expertise in the finance, health care and retail fields. Its products make it easy to launch everything from food delivery businesses and custom T-shirt design to job websites. The company, which began operations in 2011, is woman and minority owned and based in Mount Dora, Fla. Across Borders has been honored numerous times for its outstanding growth. It has been named to the Inc. 5000 list three times and made the Orlando Business Journal’s Fast 50 in 2016 and 2017. It has also been named among Florida Companies to Watch by GrowFL.
