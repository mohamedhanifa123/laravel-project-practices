Kandice Caines

HR & Operations Manager

Kandice Caines joined the Integrass Team in November 2015. She handles Benefits, assists with Payroll, and guides the Operations Department. Kandice is also a State certified Notary for the state of Florida. She is a valued team member with a “can do” attitude, who continues to be an asset to the constantly growing team in this competitive industry.

Kandice comes with the following credentials of a Bachelor’s Degreein Marketing and a Master’s Degree in Human Resources Management.
