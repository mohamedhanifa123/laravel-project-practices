Ted J. Spero

Advisory Board Member

Ted J. Spero, a leader in the Information Technology industry for over 20 years, holds the position of Vice President of IT Infrastructure at FAIRWINDS Credit Union, where he has worked since 2005. FAIRWINDS is the largest financial institution headquartered in Central Florida with over $2.3 billion in assets and 190,000 members.

Prior to joining FAIRWINDS, Ted performed IT consulting services for the United States Department of Defense and supervised the global data center team of Ocwen Financial Corporation, one of the largest mortgage servicers in the United States. Ted has spoken at numerous events including the FDIC Seidman Center in Arlington, VA, NCUA IS&T SME Conference in Miami, FL, and the ToolCASE Client Conference in Greenwood Village, CO on topics including Virtualization of Oracle databases, Virtualization security, and performance optimization for virtualized Oracle OLTP workloads. Ted has also been interviewed for published articles in Bank Systems & Technology, InformationWeek, and others.

Ted has a Bachelor of Science Degree in Information Technology and a Master of Business Administration Degree from the University of Central Florida.
