Wendy Martinez

HR & Accounts Manager

Wendy Martinez joined the Integrass team in January 2020. Wendy has several years of experience in the Operations area, including Human Resources. She is a great team player who enjoys multitasking. She assists with Finance, Human Resources, Operations, and Employee relations.

Wendy holds a degree in Criminal Justice and Forensics Technologies from the ITT Technical Institute in New Orleans, Louisiana.
